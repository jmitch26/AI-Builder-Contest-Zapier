About the Contest

What Is Zapier's AI Builder Contest?

We're inviting people everywhere to show us what they've built with AI. Ideally, your submission showcases something that helps you operate at a higher level at work — but if AI isn't available in your workplace, we're open to other use cases that have had a meaningful impact on your life or workflow.

🤖

AI at the Core

Your submission should showcase what you've actually built with AI — not just prompted. Basic insight generation or one-off prompts won't cut it. We want to see AI connected to tools, integrated into workflows, and meaningfully improving a real process from end to end.

📈

Sales Impact Preferred

We'd love to see builds tied to sales motions — prospecting, outreach, pipeline management, customer engagement, or closing deals more effectively. That said, if AI isn't available in your workplace, we're open to other use cases that have had meaningful impact.

🤖
Zapier Company Value

Build the Robot

One of Zapier's core values is Build the Robot — the belief that if you're doing something repetitive, you should automate it. We don't just sell automation, we live it. This contest is a direct expression of that value: we want to see what people are actually building with AI — creating systems that do the tedious work so humans can focus on what only humans can do.

Requirements

What Your Build Needs

Both boxes must be checked for your submission to qualify.

Requirement 01

Sales-Focused

Your build must be directly applicable to a sales use case. Think: prospecting automation, personalized outreach, pipeline health monitoring, or anything that moves deals forward.

Requirement 02

AI-Powered

AI must play a meaningful role — not just a checkbox. This could be a language model, an AI agent, AI-generated content, or intelligent decision-making built into the workflow.

Judging

How We'll Evaluate Entries

Submissions are assessed against Zapier's AI Fluency Rubric — a structured framework that measures real-world AI capability.

Dimension What We're Looking For
AI Integration Depth How meaningfully is AI incorporated? Surface-level prompting vs. agentic, multi-step workflows.
Sales Relevance Does this directly improve a sales outcome? Clear connection to pipeline, revenue, or productivity.
Innovation & Creativity Does this solve a real problem in a novel way? We reward original thinking.
Clarity of Demo Can we understand what you built and why it matters from your video and summary?

Who Can Enter

Prior to prize distribution, winners must meet with the organizing team and verify their identity. We need to confirm you are who you say you are before any prizes are awarded.

No Fraud

Submissions must represent your own original work. Any attempt to game the contest, misrepresent authorship, or submit fraudulent entries will result in immediate disqualification.

How to Submit

What to Include

Fill out the submission form with the following information. All fields are required.

  • Your Name — First and last name as it appears officially.
  • Email Address — We'll use this to contact you if you win.
  • LinkedIn Profile — Public profile URL so we can verify your professional identity.
  • Video Demo — A publicly accessible link showing your build in action.
  • Build Summary — A short written description of what you built and the problem it solves.
  • Tools Used — List every tool, app, or AI model included in your build.
